Tower Defense Simulator

Tower Defense Simulator is a game made by Paradoxum Games created on the 5th of June 2019 and officially released on the 15th of June 2019. It involves players teaming up with one another to fighting waves of different enemies until they either are overrun or triumph that particular map. Players gain cash by damaging enemies and from wave bonuses, which can subsequently be invested in buying new towers or upgrading existing ones. Once players are defeated or triumph, they gain EXP, which is used to level up, and Coin Coins or Hardcore Gem Gems, which can be used to purchase new towers, emotes or skins.
